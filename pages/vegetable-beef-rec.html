Vegetable Beef Soup

Back to Home

Ingredients


  • 1 white onion
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 3 large carrots
  • 2 large potatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 lb stewing beef (or chuck steak, cut into cubes)
  • 3 cups low sodium beef stock
  • 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 4 Tbsp (1/4 cup) flour
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

  • Buttery Mushrooms (optional)
    • 1 Tbsp butter
    • 6 oz white mushrooms, sliced

Instructions


  1. Peel and cut potatoes into 1/2-inch cubes. Peel and slice carrots into 1/4-inch slices. Finely chop onion and celery. Mince garlic.
  2. Heat oil in large pot over high heat.
  3. Pat beef dry with paper towels, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. Brown beef aggressively, then remove beef and set aside.
  5. Add garlic and onion, cook for 2 minutes until fragrant and onion is translucent.
  6. Stir in flour, then slowly pour in beef broth while constantly stirring.
  7. Add vinegar, water, tomato paste, bay leaves and thyme, stir well.
  8. Add the beef back into pot.
  9. Cover, reduce heat to medium low and simmer 1 hour 15 minutes.
  10. Add potatoes and simmer for 20 more minutes without the lid. Add cooked mushrooms (optional) in the last 5 minutes.
  11. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve.

  • Buttery Mushrooms (optional)
    1. Melt butter in a medium skillet over medium high heat.
    2. Add mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes until browned. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
