Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Back to Home

Ingredients


  • 1 Tbsp + 4 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 medium sweet yellow onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 to 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 large carrots, peeled and cut into thin slices
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 cups low sodium chicken stock
  • 2 cups fat free half & half
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper to taste (optional but recommended)
  • 8 oz grated high quality extra sharp cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions


  1. Dice onion, mince garlic, and peel and cut carrots into thin slices.
  2. In a medium pan, add 1 Tbsp butter and diced onion. Sauté over medium heat until onion is translucent and barely browned, about 4 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and cook about 30 seconds, stirring constantly. Remove pan from heat and set aside.
  4. In a large pot, add remaining butter and flour and cook over medium heat for 3-5 minutes, whisking constantly, until flour is thickened.
  5. Slowly add chicken stock, whisking constantly.
  6. Slowly add half & half, whisking constantly.
  7. Allow mixture to simmer over low heat for 15-20 minutes, whisking intermittently to re-incorporate skin.
  8. After simmering 15-20 minutes, add broccoli, carrots, and onion and garlic previously set aside.
  9. Add salt, pepper, paprika, cayenne pepper and stir to combine.
  10. Allow to simmer over low heat for 20-25 minutes, whisking intermittently to re-incorporate skin.
  11. While soup simmers, grate the cheese. After simmering 20-25 minutes, add most of the cheese, reserving a small amount for garnishing if desired. Stir in cheese until melted and fully incorporated, about 1 minute.
  12. Transfer to bowls and serve.
