Lemony Chicken Spaghetti

Back to Home

Ingredients


  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 lemon
  • 6 oz spaghetti
  • 10 oz chicken cutlets
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp cooking oil
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1 cube chicken stock concentrate
  • 2 Tbsp sour cream
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions


  1. Halve zucchini lengthwise and cut into 1/2 inch pieces. Quarter and zest lemon. Mince garlic. Slice chicken into strips or bite sized pieces.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  3. Add spaghetti and cook 9-11 minutes.
  4. Reserve 1 cup pasta cooking water, then drain.
  5. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high, add zucchini and cook until zucchini is browned and softened, 4-6 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Transfer zucchini to a plate and wipe out pan.
  7. Meanwhile, season chicken all over with Italian seasoning, salt and pepper.
  8. Once zucchini is done, heat a drizzle of cooking oil in same pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through.
  9. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in pot used for spaghetti over medium-high heat. Add garlic, half the lemon zest and a pinch of chili flakes. Cook, stirring until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  10. Stir in 1/2 cup reserved pasta cooking water, stock concentrate, and juice from two lemon wedges. Simmer until thickened, 1-2 minutes. Turn off heat.
  11. Add drained spaghetti, zucchini, sour cream, a 1 Tbsp butter to pot with sauce, toss to coat.
  12. Add half the Parmesan and season with salt and pepper.
  13. Divide pasta between bowls, top with chicken, remaining Parmesan, remaining lemon zest, and a pinch of chili flakes. Serve with remaining lemon wedges on the side.
