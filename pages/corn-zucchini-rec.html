Corn and Zucchini Sauté

Back to Home

Ingredients


  • 2 cans whole kernel corn
  • 1 medium zucchini
  • 1.5 tsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1/2 tsp white-wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions


  1. Drain corn. Halve zucchini lengthwise and slice into 1/4 inch pieces. Mince garlic.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add zucchini and garlic and cook 1-2 minutes until zucchini is bright green.
  4. Add corn and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook, tossing, until corn is heated through, 2-3 minutes.
  6. Removed from heat, stir in basil and white-wine vinegar and serve.
