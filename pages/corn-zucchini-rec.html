Ingredients


  • 2 cans whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 medium zucchini, halved lengthwise and thinly sliced
  • 1.5 tsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1/2 tsp white-wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions


  1. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add zucchini and garlic and cook 1-2 minutes until zucchini is bright green.
  3. Add corn and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook, tossing, until corn is heated through, 2-3 minutes.
  5. Removed from heat, stir in basil and white-wine vinegar and serve.
