Ingredients


  • 2 scallions
  • 1 zucchini
  • 4 oz grape tomatoes
  • 3/4 cup arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable stock
  • 2-3 tsp basil oil
  • 2-3 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp Tuscan Heat Spice
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions


  1. Trim and thinly slice scallions, separating whites from greens. Halve tomatoes. Trim and dice zucchini into 1/2-inch pieces.
  2. Heat a drizzle of cooking oil into a large pan over medium heat. Add scallion whites, cook until softened, 1 minute.
  3. Add rice and 2 tsp Tuscan Heat Spice. Stir until rice is translucent, 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add 1/2 cup of stock, stirring until liquid has mostly absorbed. Repeat 1/2 cup at a time, until rice is al dente and mixture is creamy, 25-30 minutes.
  5. Once rice has cooked 20 minutes, heat 1 tsp basil oil in a separate large pan over medium-high heat. Add zucchini and 1 tsp Tuscan Heat Spice. Cook, stirring, until tender and lightly browned, 4-6 minutes.
  6. Add tomatoes and 2-3 tsp balsamic vinegar to pan with zucchini. Cook, stirring, until tomatoes have softened, 2-3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper and turn off heat.
  7. Once risotto is done, stir in 1 Tbsp butter and half the Parmesan. Drizzle with 1-2 tsp of basil oil to taste and serve.
