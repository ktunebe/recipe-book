Quinoa Bowls

Back to Home

Ingredients


  • 1/2 cup quinoa
  • 1 head lettuce
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breast or frozen cooked shrimp
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Instructions


  1. In a small pot, bring 1 cup water to a boil.
  2. Add quinoa, reduce heat to low, simmer until water is absorbed.
  3. While water comes to a boil, cut chicken into chunks or thaw and de-tail shrimp.
  4. In a large bowl, mix olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, thyme, black pepper and cayenne pepper.
  5. Add chicken or shrimp to mixture and stir to coat.
  6. In a medium pan, cook over medium heat until chicken is cooked or shrimp is heated through.
  7. While cooking, shred lettuce and place into bowls. Halve and cut avocado into thin slices. Place avocado and broccoli florets over lettuce.
  8. Once cooked, top bowls with quinoa and chicken or shrimp and serve with dressing of choice.
