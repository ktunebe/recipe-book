Vegetable Beef Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb stewing beef (or chuck steak, cut into cubes)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 white onion, chopped fine
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 stalks celery, sliced or chopped
  • 3 large carrots, peeled and cut into 1/4 inch slices
  • 4 tbsp (1/4 cup) flour
  • 3 cups low sodium beef stock
  • 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes

  • Buttery Mushrooms (optional)
    • 1 tbsp butter
    • 6 oz small mushrooms, sliced

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in large pot over high heat.
  2. Pat beef dry with paper towels, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  3. Brown beef aggressively, then remove beef and set aside.
  4. Add garlic and onion, cook for 2 minutes until fragrant and onion is translucent.
  5. Stir in flour, then slowly pour in beef broth while constantly stirring.
  6. Add vinegar, water, tomato paste, bay leaves and thyme, stir well.
  7. Add the beef back into pot.
  8. Cover, reduce heat to medium low and simmer 1 hour 15 minutes.
  9. Add potatoes and simmer for 20 more minutes without the lid. Add cooked mushrooms (optional) in the last 5 minutes.
  10. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve.

  • Buttery Mushrooms (optional)
    1. Melt butter in a medium skillet over medium high heat.
    2. Add mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes until browned. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
