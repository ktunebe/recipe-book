Pan-Fried Pork Chops

Back to Home

Ingredients


  • 2 bone-in pork chops, about 1 inch thick each
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • Fresh Thyme sprigs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions


  1. Preheat oven to 400°.
  2. Season pork chops with salt and pepper.
  3. Melt butter in an oven-proof skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add pork chops and a few springs of thyme and sear until chops are golden, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Transfer pork chops in skillet to oven and cook through, about 12 minutes (Pork is safe at 145°).
  6. Serve immediately.
