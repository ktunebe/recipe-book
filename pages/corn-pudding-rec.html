Corn Pudding

Back to Home

Ingredients


  • 2 cans whole corn
  • 2 cans cream corn
  • 2 sticks butter
  • 2 8 oz packages sour cream
  • 2 boxes Jiffy cornbread mix

Instructions


  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. In a large bowl, melt butter.
  3. Drain corn and mix with butter and sour cream.
  4. Sprinkle in cornbread mix and mix well.
  5. Transfer to large baking dish.
  6. Bake for 1 hour.
  7. Remove from oven when you can stick a knife into pudding and it comes out clean.
