Tortellini Soup

Back to Home

Ingredients


  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 2 large carrots
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 1 28 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 3 1/2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 Tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1/2 tsp salt, plus more to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 9-12 oz cheese tortellini

Instructions


  1. Peel and slice carrots into 1/4 inch pieces. Dice onion. Mince garlic.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, salt and a few grinds of pepper. Cook, stirring, until vegetables begin to soften, about 8 minutes.
  3. Add balsamic vinegar, garlic, tomatoes, broth, thyme and red pepper flakes. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the tortellini in a pot of boiling water according to the package directions until al dente.
  5. Add tortellini to soup and simmer for 2 more minutes.
  6. Season to taste with more salt and pepper and serve.
