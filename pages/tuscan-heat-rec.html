Tuscan Heat Spice Mix

Back to Home

Ingredients


  • 4 tsp basil
  • 2 tsp rosemary
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper

Instructions


  1. Mix all spices together thoroughly.
  2. Store in an air-tight container up to 6 months.
