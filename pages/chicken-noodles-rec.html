Chicken and Noodles

Back to Home

Ingredients


  • 1/2 stick butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 2-4 cups water
  • Egg noodles or Amish flat noodles
  • 2 tsp thyme
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp parsely
  • 1 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 Tbsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp paprika (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 rotisserie chicken

Instructions


  1. Melt butter in large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add flour, whisk until combined, and brown.
  3. Slowly add chicken stock, whisking constantly.
  4. Add between 2-4 cups water for desired consistency.
  5. Bring to a boil, whisking frequently to keep roux from clumping.
  6. Add noodles.
  7. Cook according to noodle packaging, stirring occasionally, adding spices in the last minute or two.
  8. While noodles are cooking, shred white meat from rotisserie chicken.
  9. Stir chicken into noodles after removing from heat
  10. Serve in bowls.
