Al Bundy's Chili

Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 1 white onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green pepper, ribs and seeds removed and finely chopped
  • 2 jalapeños, ribs and seeds removed and finely chopped
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 14 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups beef stock
  • 1 cup instant white rice
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1 Tbsp paprika
  • 1/2 Tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 Tbsp oregano
  • 1/2 Tbsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a drizzle of oil in large pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook garlic, onions, green peppers, and jalapeños 2-3 minutes, until fragrant and onion is translucent.
  3. Add ground beef, chili powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, oregano, cumin, salt and pepper.
  4. Cook until beef is browned through.
  5. Add beef stock and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  7. While chili simmers, bring 1 cup water to a boil in a small pot.
  8. Add rice and remove from heat.
  9. Serve chili over rice.
