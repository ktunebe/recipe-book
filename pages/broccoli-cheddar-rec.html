Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp + 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 medium sweet yellow onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 cups low sodium chicken stock
  • 2 cups fat free half & half
  • 3 to 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 large carrots, peeled and cut into thin slices
  • 3/4 tsp salt or to taste
  • 3/4 tsp black pepper or to taste
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper to taste (optional but recommended)
  • 8 oz grated high quality extra sharp cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. In a medium pan, add 1 tbsp butter and diced onion. Sauté over medium heat until onion is translucent and barely browned, about 4 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and cook about 30 seconds, stirring constantly. Remove pan from heat and set aside.
  3. In a large pot, add remaining butter and flour and cook over medium heat for 3-5 minutes, whisking constantly, until flour is thickened.
  4. Slowly add chicken stock, whisking constantly.
  5. Slowly add half & half, whisking constantly.
  6. Allow mixture to simmer over low heat for 15-20 minutes, whisking intermittently to re-incorporate skin.
  7. After simmering 15-20 minutes, add broccoli, carrots, and onion and garlic previously set aside.
  8. Add salt, pepper, paprika, cayenne pepper and stir to combine.
  9. Allow to simmer over low heat for 20-25 minutes, whisking intermittently to re-incorporate skin.
  10. While soup simmers, grate the cheese. After simmering 20-25 minutes, add most of the cheese, reserving a small amount for garnishing if desired. Stir in cheese until melted and fully incorporated, about 1 minute.
  11. Transfer to bowls and serve.
