Chicken Ramen

Back to Home

Ingredients


  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 thumb ginger
  • 2 scallions
  • 6 oz button or white mushrooms
  • 10 oz chicken cutlets
  • 6 oz ramen noodles
  • 1 Tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 2 chicken bullion cubes
  • 2 beef bullion cubes
  • Crispy fried onions for topping
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions


  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Peel and mince garlic and ginger. Thinly slice mushrooms. Thinly slice scallions and separate whites from greens.
  3. Slice chicken into strips or bite-sized pieces.
  4. In a small bowl, combine sesame seeds, half the garlic, half the sesame oil, a drizzle of cooking oil, chili flakes, and a big pinch of salt. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and microwave for 30 seconds. Set aside.
  5. Once water is boiling, add ramen noodles and cook according to package. Drain, then toss noodles with a drizzle of cooking oil. Set aside.
  6. Heat a drizzle of cooking oil in pot used for noodles over medium-high heat. Add scallion whites, ginger and remaining garlic and cook stirring for 30 seconds.
  7. Stir in 4 cups water, bullion cubes, and soy sauce. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce heat to low.
  8. Meanwhile, heat a large drizzle of cooking oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Drizzle with remaining sesame oil and mix to coat, then transfer chicken to a cutting board.
  9. Add mushrooms and a pinch of salt to pan used for chicken over medium-high heat. Cook, stirring occasionally, until browned and softened, about 4-5 minutes.
  10. Divide ramen noodles between bowls. Pour broth over noodles and stir in desired amount of chili-garlic oil. Top with chicken, mushrooms, scallion greens and crispy onions and serve.
